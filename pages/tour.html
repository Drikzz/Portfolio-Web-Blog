#Day_6

Day Six: Jacket who you?? ü•∂

June 13, 2025 ‚Äî We traveled 6 hours to the Summer Capital of the Philippines - Baguio City. We arrived 5am in the morning, we didn't not expect the cold weather, so we were not prepared for it. We already had our tour jackets, but it was not still not enough to keep us warm. We first visted Strawberry Farm and bought strawberry taho, some ube ice cream, and some pasalubong.
